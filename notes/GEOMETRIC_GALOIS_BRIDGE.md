# Geometric-Galois Bridge

This note records the mainstream bridge for the canonical-lane package.

Mainstream precursors:
- motivic realizations, regulator maps, and special-value comparison formulas give the standard bridge language.
- Galois/cohomological realizations and L-value identities supply the ordinary endpoint observables.
- the lane adds admissible transport, tracked defect ledgers, and an explicit remainder complement instead of silently extrapolating beyond the admissible motivic sector.

Problem-side translation:
- `P_mc` projects the visible object to the admissible sector `Y_mc^BEI` tracked by the lane.
- `Q_rem := I - P_mc` records the explicit remainder `R_BEI` carried by the defect and coherence ledgers.
- the manuscript closes on `Y_mc^BEI`; any stronger external consequence must control `R_BEI` rather than dropping it.
